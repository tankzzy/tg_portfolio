    Our School

    Breakforth Academy is a progressive educational institution
    that prioritizes the well-being and holistic development of its
    students. The school's curriculum is designed to promote
    critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills, while
    also emphasizing physical and emotional wellness.

    Why Choose Us

    what we do

    Our curriculum is designed to be engaging and challenging, while
    also promoting creativity and critical thinking. We offer a range of
    subjects, including math, science, language arts, social studies, and
    the arts. We also offer extracurricular programs

    Our Mission

    At breakforth Academy, our mission is to inspire a love of learning
    and help our students develop the skills they need to become
    responsible, engaged citizens. To bring out the greatness in every
    child and to prepare and equip children in readiness for tomorrow.

    Our Goal

    we believe that a strong sense of community is essential to student
    success. We foster a warm and welcoming school culture, where
    students, families, and educators work together to create a positive
    and supportive environment.
